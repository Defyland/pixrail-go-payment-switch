# Personas

## Fintech Platform Engineer

Needs a clear boundary for Pix transfer intake, idempotency, provider adapters, and event publication. Cares about code structure, failure behavior, and maintainability.

## Risk Operations Lead

Needs fraud scores, triggered rules, receiver risk evidence, and audit records that explain every approve, block, and review decision.

## SRE

Needs health, readiness, domain metrics, alertable failure modes, outbox retry visibility, runbooks, and bounded operational cost.

## Financial Core Team

Needs PixRail to emit reliable payment-rail events without claiming ledger ownership. Cares about not duplicating settlement, balance, or reconciliation logic.
